Ducks

Ducks are water-loving birds with webbed feet that help them swim easily.
They come in many colors and sizes, often found in ponds, lakes, and rivers.
Ducks make a variety of sounds, from soft quacks to loud calls.
They're also adaptive to swim, walk, and fly.

  • A duck's feathers are waterproof, keeping them dry even while swimming
  • Some ducks can sleep with one eye open to stay alert for predators.
